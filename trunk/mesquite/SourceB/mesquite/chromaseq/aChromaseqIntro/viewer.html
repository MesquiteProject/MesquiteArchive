Chromatogram Viewer

Mesquite Chromaseq has a basic chromatogram viewer built in. To see the chromatgrams for a particular sequence, touch the View Chromatograms tool () on a sequence. The chromatogram viewer should appear, centered approximately on the base you touched.

Here is a section of the chromatogram viewer:

Note that only the center line will have bases lined up one read to the next; off center they may not line up.

There are various tools for navigating quickly through the chromatograms, changing the display of the chromatograms. You can also change the base calls.
